Who I Am

Creative Professional | CMO | Beauty Expert | Social Activist

I am Vishalini Karikaran, a free-spirited, curious, and fiercely independent individual based in Kandy, Sri Lanka. My journey bridges the worlds of social activism, cosmetics, structured marketing, and creative filmmaking. I thrive in environments where creativity meets purpose.

My Vision

To build a startup that places Eelam Tamil culture on the world map, blending rich traditions with modern entrepreneurial success. I believe in narratives that uplift communities and storytelling that resonates globally.

Currently serving as the Chief Marketing Officer at Newborn Cinema, I lead strategies that amplify Tamil film productions. Before diving into the cinematic world, I spent four years as an NGO Coordinator, focusing on community programs, empowerment, and social development. To complement my strategic endeavors, I actively hone my artistry as a Freeman Hairdressing & Beauty Expert.
